Research Areas

Multimodal AI Safety & Privacy

We lead the development of next-generation frameworks for multimodal privacy detection, high-risk content understanding, and safe generative AI. Our projects include large-scale PII datasets, de-identification systems, and the geolocation privacy benchmark KoreaGEO.

Benchmarking & Evaluation Science

We build rigorous evaluation systems for large language models, focusing on cultural reasoning, politeness, figurative language, safety robustness, and linguistic bias. Our Korean-centered benchmarks set new standards for trustworthy and culturally aware AI.

Korean NLP & Linguistic Resources

We construct large-scale linguistic resources, domain corpora, learner datasets, and historical archives that serve as foundational infrastructure for both modern AI and Korean linguistic research.

AI for Korean Language Education

Our research advances automated writing assessment, pronunciation evaluation, educational chatbots, and corpus-driven pedagogy to support learners of Korean worldwide.
