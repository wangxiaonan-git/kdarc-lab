Research Areas

Multimodal AI Safety & Privacy

Frameworks for multimodal privacy detection, high-risk content understanding, PII datasets, de-identification systems, and geolocation privacy analysis.

Benchmarking & Evaluation Science

Rigorous evaluation methods for LLMs focusing on cultural reasoning, politeness, figurative language, safety robustness, and linguistic bias.

Korean NLP & Linguistic Resources

Large-scale corpora, learner datasets, historical archives, and foundational resources for Korean linguistic and AI research.

AI for Language Education

Automated writing assessment, pronunciation evaluation, educational AI systems, and corpus-driven learning analytics.

Cross-cultural & Applied NLP

Cross-lingual alignment, East Asian language processing, cultural bias analysis, and applied NLP for real-world domains.
